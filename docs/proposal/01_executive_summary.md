## The Challenge

Electric vehicles are transforming global transportation, yet battery management remains the Achilles' heel of EV adoption. Current Battery Management Systems (BMS) are reactive, cloud-dependent, and lack lifecycle transparency. Thermal runaway incidents cost manufacturers billions, battery degradation reduces vehicle resale value, and the absence of standardized battery identity hinders recycling and second-life applications.

## Our Solution

**EdgeTwin-BMS+** is a first-of-its-kind Edge AI platform that unifies **TinyML inference**, **Digital Twin visualization**, **Battery Passport**, and **Explainable AI** into a single, cloud-independent system. Every battery gets a digital identity, every cell gets real-time monitoring, and every prediction comes with an explanation.

## Technology Pillars

1. **TinyML on ESP32** — Physics-informed CNN-LSTM hybrid running TensorFlow Lite Micro with INT8 quantization
2. **Digital Twin** — Real-time 3D battery pack visualization with cell-level risk coding and failure simulation
3. **Battery Passport** — EU Battery Regulation-compliant digital identity tracking 17 lifecycle attributes
4. **Explainable AI** — SHAP/LIME integration providing transparent, auditable predictions
5. **Federated Learning** — Privacy-preserving fleet-wide model improvement without data sharing
